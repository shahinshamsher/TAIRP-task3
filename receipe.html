Delicious Dish

Ingredients:

  • coffee 1
  • ice cube 2
  • milk 3

Instructions:

  1. Step 1: Firstly, add 1 tablespoon of instant coffee to a bowl. If using brewed filter coffee or espresso you can add ¼ cup directly to the blender jar
  2. Step 2: Add ¼ cup of warm water (not hot) to the instant coffee. Stir and mix very well.
  3. Step 3: Add the coffee solution to a blender jar.
